# GLOSA — Name Origin

**Selected**: GLOSA (*gloss / marginal annotation*)

From the medieval manuscript tradition where scholars wrote explanatory notes in the margins of texts. In Spanish literature, *glosas* evolved into a poetic form where a poet takes existing lines and expands them with commentary and interpretation.

The score directives are literally glosses — marginal annotations that explain how to interpret the text. The literary form (expanding terse lines into rich interpretation) mirrors LLM-driven instruct synthesis: terse GLOSA tags become rich natural-language performance direction.
